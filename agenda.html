Tournament Day Agenda

7:00 AM

Check-in

Arrive at Canyon Springs Golf Club at 7:00 AM for check-in. Players will receive a goodie bag with a drink and lunch ticket. Players will also receive a bucket of range balls to begin warming up.

7:15 AM

Range open for practice and pre-tournament warm up

The range will be open from 7:00 - 7:45 AM. The range includes a practice area for chipping and putting.

7:45 AM

Call to start and tournament director commencement speach

Players should wrap up their practice and gather in their foursomes. The tournament director will announce a commencement speach and send teams to their starting holes to for a shotgun start.

8:00 AM

Shotgun Start

All teams will begin at 8:00 AM from their repective holes.

12:00 - 1:00 PM

BBQ Lunch and Raffle
